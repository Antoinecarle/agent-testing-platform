# Copywriting Formulas

## AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

1. **Attention**: Bold headline or surprising stat
2. **Interest**: Explain the problem/opportunity
3. **Desire**: Show the solution and social proof
4. **Action**: Clear CTA with low friction

## PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

1. **Problem**: Name the specific pain point
2. **Agitate**: Make them feel the cost of inaction
3. **Solution**: Present your product as the answer

## BAB (Before-After-Bridge)

1. **Before**: Current painful state
2. **After**: Desired future state
3. **Bridge**: Your product is how they get there

## 4 Cs of Effective Copy

1. **Clear**: No jargon, simple words
2. **Concise**: Cut ruthlessly (aim for 50% of first draft)
3. **Compelling**: Why should they care?
4. **Credible**: Back claims with proof

## Power Words by Category

| Emotion | Words |
|---------|-------|
| Urgency | Now, today, instant, limited, before |
| Trust | Proven, guaranteed, secure, certified |
| Exclusivity | Premium, exclusive, members-only, invitation |
| Simplicity | Easy, simple, effortless, one-click, automatic |
| Value | Free, save, bonus, unlimited, included |
