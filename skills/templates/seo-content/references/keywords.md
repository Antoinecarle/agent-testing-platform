# Keyword Research Patterns

## Keyword Types

| Type | Example | Intent |
|------|---------|--------|
| Head | "SEO" | Informational |
| Long-tail | "how to improve SEO for small business" | Informational |
| Transactional | "buy SEO tools" | Commercial |
| Local | "SEO agency Paris" | Local |
| Question | "what is on-page SEO?" | Informational |

## Keyword Clustering

Group related keywords by topic cluster:

**Pillar**: "SEO guide"
- Cluster: "on-page SEO", "technical SEO", "link building"
- Cluster: "keyword research", "content optimization"
- Cluster: "local SEO", "Google Business Profile"

## Search Intent Mapping

| Intent | Content Type | CTA |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Informational | Blog post, guide | Newsletter signup |
| Navigational | Landing page | Direct link |
| Commercial | Comparison, review | Free trial |
| Transactional | Product page | Buy now |

## Content Gaps

Look for keywords where:
- Competitors rank but you don't
- Search volume > 100/mo with KD < 30
- Featured snippet opportunities (question queries)
- Reddit/forum discussions without authoritative answers
